About the Municipality

The Municipality of Ramos is a fourth-class municipality in the Province of Tarlac. It is committed to promoting good governance, sustainable development, and quality public service for all Ramosians.

History

Ramos was established as a municipality on January 1, 1921, named after President Fidel V. Ramos. It has since grown into a peaceful agricultural community.

Geography

Ramos is located in the central part of Tarlac, composed of several barangays with vast agricultural lands and residential areas.
